Basic concepts on:<br>
Numerical Types(integer,float,Complex)<br>

Arithmetic Operators:<br>
'+' Addition: Adds two operands.<br>
'-' Subtraction: Subtracts the right operand from the left operand.<br>
'*' Multiplication: Multiplies two operands.<br>
'/' Division: Divides the left operand by the right operand.<br>
'%' Modulus: Returns the remainder of dividing the left operand by the right operand.<br>
'**' Exponentiation: Raises the left operand to the power of the right operand.<br>
'//' Floor Division: Divides the left operand by the right operand and returns the largest
integer less than or equal to the result.<br>

Type Conversion(int(),float(),str())<br>

Comparision Operators:<br>
'==' Equal to: True if both operands are equal.<br>
'!=' Not equal to: True if operands are not equal.<br>
'>' Greater than: True if the left operand is greater than the right operand.<br>
'<' Less than: True if the left operand is less than the right operand.<br>
'>=' Greater than or equal to: True if the left operand is greater than or equal to the right
operand.<br>
'<=' Less than or equal to: True if the left operand is less than or equal to the right operand<br>
